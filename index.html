Awesome Alfred Workflows

A curated collection of awesome Alfred 3 workflows.

This is being written by the guidelines from awesome. However, there might be some misguided contents even with our best effort since we are new in this Awesome movement and still learning.

Web

  • Chrome Bookmarks makes you access to your Chrome bookmarks on your fingertips:

    ,b

Finder

  • Autojump is Finder version of autojump. With this you can navigate your filesystem and open the folder you want into Finder:

    j

Development

  • Secure SHell opens SSH/SFTP connections with autosuggestion of known hosts:

    ssh

  • Show Vim Cheatsheet shows a cheatsheet for vim using quicklook. Simple and useful:

    vi

  • Tunnelblick Toggle checks and toggles the status of Tunnelblick connections:

    vpn
